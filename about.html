Our Mission

Youth Network was founded in 2015 with a simple but powerful vision: to create opportunities for young people to develop the skills, confidence, and connections they need to thrive in an ever-changing world.

We believe every young person deserves access to quality education, mentorship, and resources regardless of their background or circumstances. Our programs bridge the gap between traditional education and the real-world skills needed for success.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our organization's history

2015

Founded with a Vision

Youth Network was established by a group of educators and community leaders to address the growing need for practical youth development programs.

2016

First Programs Launched

Piloted our initial digital skills and leadership programs, serving 200 youth in our first year.

2018

Expanded to 5 Cities

With growing demand, we expanded our programs to serve youth across multiple communities.

2020

Pandemic Response

Rapidly adapted all programs to virtual formats, ensuring continuity during challenging times.

2022

10,000 Youth Served

Celebrated reaching our 10,000th program participant with a special community event.

2023

National Recognition

Received the National Youth Empowerment Award for our innovative programs.
